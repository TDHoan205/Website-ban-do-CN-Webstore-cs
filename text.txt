
PHAN TICH HE THONG AI CHATBOT

1) MO HINH (MODEL) DANG DUNG
- Chat: Groq Cloud API (OpenAI-compatible), model: llama-3.3-70b-versatile.
- Embedding: Gemini Embedding API, model: gemini-embedding-001 (768 chieu).
 
2) KIEN TRUC VA THUAT TOAN CHINH
- Pipeline: User -> IntentDetection -> RAGEngine -> AIAgent -> GroqService -> Response.
- IntentDetection: Rule-based keyword matching (tap tu khoa) de phan loai y dinh.
- RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation):
	* Build knowledge base tu Products va FAQs.
	* Chia van ban thanh chunk (toi da ~500 ky tu) de tang ngu canh.
	* Tao embedding vector cho tung chunk.
	* Tim kiem tuong dong bang cosine similarity.
	* Co them lexical boost (khop chuoi) va priority boost (uu tien theo priority).
- AI Agent:
	* Tao system prompt theo role (customer/admin).
	* Goi chat model (Groq) de sinh cau tra loi.
	* Co fallback rule-based neu AI loi hoac khong co ket qua.
- Tool Dispatcher (function calling gia lap):
	* Parse response theo JSON pattern va regex ngon ngu tu nhien.
	* Co the goi cac hanh dong: add_to_cart, check_inventory, search_products.

3) NHUNG PHAN DA LAM DUOC
- Chat AI 24/7 cho khach hang va admin.
- Nhan dien y dinh (greeting, purchase, inquiry, payment, warranty, shipping, ...).
- RAG lay tri thuc tu DB (FAQ + san pham) de tra loi co ngu canh.
- Goi y san pham dua tren ngu canh va bo loc gia/loai.
- Co co che fallback khi AI that bai.
- Co xu ly tool call co ban de tu dong them gio/kiem tra ton kho.

4) NHUNG PHAN CAN PHAT TRIEN THEM
- Embedding/RAG:
	* Dung vector DB (vd: pgvector, Qdrant) de truy van nhanh va chinh xac hon.
	* Them reranking (cross-encoder) de tang do chinh xac cho top-k.
	* Tang metadata filter (hang, thuong hieu, gia, tinh trang kho, rating).
- Chat/Prompting:
	* Nho dai hoi thoai (session memory) va tom tat ngu canh.
	* Personalization theo lich su xem/mua hang.
	* Guardrails: kiem soat hallucination, an toan noi dung.
- Tool calling:
	* Thay regex bang schema-based tool calling, co xac nhan hanh dong quan trong.
	* Logging va theo doi ty le thanh cong cua tool call.
- Danh gia chat luong:
	* Luu log QA, feedback nguoi dung.
	* Tinh diem huu ich, phat hien cau tra loi sai, cai thien prompt.
- Hieu nang va chi phi:
	* Cache ket qua embedding va ket qua tim kiem.
	* Co che batch embedding khi build knowledge base.

KET LUAN
- He thong hien tai dung Groq cho chat va Gemini cho embedding.
- RAG dang hoat dong tot cho FAQ va san pham, co the nang cap de thong minh va on dinh hon.
